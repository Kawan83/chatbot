About Shinchan

Shinchan (Shinnosuke Nohara) is a 5-year-old boy who lives in Kasukabe, Japan. Known for his cheeky jokes, funny dance moves, and love for chocolate biscuits, Shinchan never fails to make everyone laugh!

His Family

  • Hiroshi Nohara — His hardworking father
  • Misae Nohara — His short-tempered but loving mother
  • Himawari Nohara — His cute baby sister
  • Shiro — His loyal dog
